Classes

There are three different classes that run throughout the week for everyone from absolute beginners and advanced dancers. Beginners can also contact us to arrange a trial class where they will be able to meet the teacher and other students and ask any questions that they might have.
All children of all abilities are welcomed from the ages of 4 upwards.
